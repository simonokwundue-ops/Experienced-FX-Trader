Quick Configuration Summary:

KEY PARAMETERS TO ADJUST:

1. QuantumConfidenceThreshold (default: 60%)
   - Most important parameter
   - Higher = fewer, higher quality trades
   - Lower = more trades, lower quality
   
2. BaseRiskPercent (default: 1.5%)
   - Risk per trade
   - Adjust based on risk tolerance
   
3. MinSignalScore (default: 60)
   - Minimum combined signal score
   - Increase for higher quality signals
   
4. MaxConcurrentPositions (default: 5)
   - Maximum open positions
   - Reduce for conservative trading

PRESET CONFIGURATIONS:

Load from Config folder:
- QuantumForexTrader_Base.set (Balanced)
- QuantumForexTrader_Scalper.set (Fast trading)
